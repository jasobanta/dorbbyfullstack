Shipping Policy

Estimated delivery time - 3-5 business days, Two Days 2 business days, Overnight delivery

Shipment cost – Depends on the Shipment Method (Charges if applicable or free delivery)

Overnight delivery is only available for the eligible order that comes under preferred location. Overnight delivery is only accessible for the product or order which is eligible according to the delivery location. The delay in delivery may occur occasionally only.

You will get a Shipment Confirmation email or number once your request has dispatched with your order tracking number(s). The tracking number of the package got active inside 24 hours.

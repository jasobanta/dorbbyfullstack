How could I see all of my order history ?

Tracking the order status is quite easy on Dorbby. You can view all of your order or your order history through your order option. If you want to track the order you check the status mentioned on the website.
If your order is in shipment then also you check the status of your order, through the link given on the website or you will also get a message on your registered number with a tracking link.
Can I check the order through Confirmation Mail?
Yes, when you order any product you get a confirmation mail and message at the same time which also consists of appropriate link through which you can check your order status. Instead of that if you are facing any difficulties in tracking your order; you can also contact our support team to get the status information.
If there is any delay in the expected delivery time of any product, then you will get informed through our support team through mail or call.

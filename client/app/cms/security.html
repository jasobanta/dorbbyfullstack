SECURE SHOPPING

Dorbby Private Limited recognizes the privacy rights of individuals or customers, and operates in compliance with privacy legislation of India. While we do acquire personal information in the normal course of business, we will never disclose, sell or lease to any third party, either free of charge or for remuneration, any personal information that you provide on this site without your permission, unless we are required to do so by law.

At Dorbby, we take the security our systems significantly, and it is our regular endeavor to make our website a safe place for our customers to browse. Yet , in the rare case when some security researcher or member of the public identifies a susceptibility in our systems, and responsibly shares the main points of it with us, we appreciate their contribution, work closely with them to address such issues with urgency.

How to report an issue?

If however, you have determined weaknesses on any of our web properties, we request to follow the steps given below:

Please contact us immediately by sending an email to security@dorbby.com with the required details to reconstruct the vulnerability scenario. This kind of may include screenshots, videos or simple text instructions.

Whenever possible, share with us your information (email, phone number), so that our security team can reach out to you if further inputs are needed to identify or close the problem.

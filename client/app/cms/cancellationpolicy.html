Cancellation Policy

The cancellation policy of Dorbby includes easy steps:

  • If you have already paid for your order, in terms of cancellation, the amount will get refunded to you.
  • A cancellation requests cannot be made after your order is shipped.
  • Only under some uncommon circumstances, Dorbby or any of our vendors can raise an order cancellation request. These circumstances could be –
  • When you ordered Product is out of stock
  • If the purchased item quantity will increase the minimum number of orders as per the terms and conditions of any on-going offers.
  • If you order any product whose price is not correctly mentioned on website
  • If there is any fraud assurance of payment occurs or even there are any suspicions about that.
  • If the mentioned address of buyers is incorrect or incomplete
  • If the order gets cancelled through merchant due to unavailability of the product or item, we would forward your order request to another seller, who can actually fulfill the requested order. In case, no seller is having your order or not able to fulfill your order and you have already done the payment, then your paid amount will get refunded as per our refund policy.

    • Refund Policy

    Want to claim for returning a product?

    We usually offer 5-10 days return guarantee on all the products, but the product you return must be in their original condition with the entire package available inside that along with its invoice. You can return any product only under these conditions:

    • Defective/Damaged Product
    • Product significantly different from the Product’s Specifications
    • Wrong Product Delivered
    • Part of Product is missing
    • Wrong Color/ Size/ Style
    • Incorrect Quantity

    The product which comes under these conditions can’t get returned:

    • Product is damaged due to misuse/overuse
    • Returned without original packaging including, price tags, labels, original packing, freebies and other accessories.
    • Defective products that are covered under Seller/Manufacturer’s warranty and reported after determining timeline.
    • Damaged products reported after 2-days.
    • Product or box is scratched due to use wrongly or minor damage because of not working of the product.
    • Product is used or worn out or changed
    • Lingerie is the category which is not eligible for return

    When do you want to replace a product?

    According to our policy, if the user wants to replace any product, they must know that their product should come under the buyer protection. The product which the user is replacing must be available in stock with the buyer. If the ordered product is out of stock the whole paid amount will get refunded. If the product which got delivered is not the same as that you have ordered then also you can replace that product.

    What is the exact timeline under which I can return my order?

    If you want to return any product, you can do that within 5-10 days of delivery. But, if you got any damaged product, then it must get informed to support team within 2 days.

    How could I return or replace the product?

    In case you want to replace or return any product, you can do that from your order history. You can request for return or replacement from them. You can also talk to our support team or write us at our mail id for the request. After your request, we will verify about the issues and one of our delivery boys will contact you for the pickup, only of your issues is valid or the product you want to replace is present in stock. After receiving the product, your claim will get processed and then return or replacement request will get processed. This whole process might take 2 weeks.

    When will I get my refund?

    We usually initiate the replacement or refund within 2 working days after we receive the products. If the refund would be done in the bank then it might take 1 to 2 week or if you have paid cash then you will get it as store credit. If you get any query regarding that you contact us on our support number or write us the email.

    How could I check the status of my refund or replacement request?

    You can easily track this status as well in the same way as that of order status. You can also write to the Dorbby Support to know about the status.
